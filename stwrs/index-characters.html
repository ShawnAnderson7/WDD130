Jango Fett

Jango Fett was a Mandalorian bounty hunter known as the best during the final years of the Galactic Republic. After the invasion of Naboo, he was hired by Darth Tyranus to serve as the genetic foundation for the Clone Army. He requested one unaltered clone to raise as his son: Boba Fett.

Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano was a Togruta Force-sensitive who left the Jedi Order. She was discovered on Shili by Jedi Master Plo Koon and became Padawan to Anakin Skywalker. After the Clone Wars, she helped form the early Rebel Alliance. She and Anakin developed a strong bond, like father and daughter.

Darth Sidious

Darth Sidious, born Sheev Palpatine, was a Sith Lord and Emperor of the Galactic Empire. As senator of Naboo, he led a double life, manipulating events to become Emperor. He orchestrated the fall of the Jedi and Republic, ruling until his death at the Battle of Endor.
